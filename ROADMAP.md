# Roadmap

Future ideas are tracked as GitHub issues. For what's already shipped, see
[CHANGELOG.md](./CHANGELOG.md).

Browse by area:

- [Theming & appearance](https://github.com/moui72/assisted-review/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+label%3Atheming)
- [Navigation & layout](https://github.com/moui72/assisted-review/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+label%3Anavigation)

- [Claude / AI](https://github.com/moui72/assisted-review/issues?q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen+label%3Aai)

## Requesting a feature

Open an issue with the `enhancement` label and describe what you want and why.
New requests are left uncategorized. A maintainer adding an area label
(`theming`, `navigation`, `review-workflow`, or `ai`) means the idea has been
accepted onto the roadmap — not that it is scheduled or committed to.
